
How to setup a Arma 2 Takistan life server

	1.Install Steam ("https://store.steampowered.com/about/")
	2.Login to steam.
	3.Install Arma 2 From steam
	4.Install Arma 2 Operation ArrowHead from steam
	5.Install Microsoft Visual C++ 2010 Redistributable Package (x86) ("https://www.microsoft.com/en-in/download/details.aspx?id=5555")
	6.Once installed launch both Arma 2 and Arma 2 Operation ArowHead and close them
	7.Then located start.bat with is in the same directoy as theeses instuctions.
	8.Launch start.bat but double clicking
	9.Now join your server and play - Rember to unblock the nesaseary ports in your firewall
	
Note:
	The laucnher will automaticly detect your arma 2 and arma 2 Operatin arrow head directoy and setup and lauch the server.
